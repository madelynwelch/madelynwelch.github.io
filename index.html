Carolina Week

Carolina's Student Newscast

Carolina Week is a weekly newscast produced, anchored and directed by students in the UNC School of Media and Journalism. The team of students produce stories each week covering the campus and community for students and residents alike. The program airs weekly on Wednesdays at 12 p.m. The show has received numerous awards including first place in the Hearst National Broadcast Championship. As the show enters its 20th season, students continue to inovate and push our platform to the next level. We are on the cutting edge of media techonology and continue to set the standard for journalism schools across the country.
