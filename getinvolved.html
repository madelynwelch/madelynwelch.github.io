Carolina Week is a weekly Hearst award winning student run newscast based out of the UNC School of Media and Journalism. The show is run by a group journalism students and is overseen by Dr. Lynn Owens and Dr. Charlie Tuggle. Each week students report on stories that affect college students and the Chapel Hill community in ways no other news outlet can. Carolina Week is a staple for those wanting to enter the news field and has sent many journalists onto successful careers at national outlets. While the course is avalaible to any student that wants to enroll, volunteers are what drive this show. If you want to learn more about how to get involved, leave your information below and we will reach out to you!
